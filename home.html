about us

The ABC institute is an established leader in the field of education, research and healthcare. In a span of over ten decades, it has transformed the lives of more than 3,00,000 students from over 59 countries. ABC was launched in 2011 on an invitation from the Government of India, as a self-financed State University. ABC has redefined academic excellence in the region, with their way of learning; one that inspires students of all disciplines to learn and innovate through hands on practical experience. ABC boasts of best-in-class infrastructure, including state-of-the-art research facilities and a modern, digital library. In line with its legacy of providing quality education to its students, the campus uses the latest in technology to impart education.
